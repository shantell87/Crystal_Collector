Crytal Collector!

Instructions
  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals given. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number provided. You lose the game if your total score above the random number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
  • Each time the game restarts the crystals will have new values.
